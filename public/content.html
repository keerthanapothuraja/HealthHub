Learning disabilities and spectrum disorders are extremely bright and above average in some areas, but struggle with academic abilities, sensory regulation, social interaction and impulsivity control.
There is usually a weakness in an area or areas of the brain that are causing the disruption of processing. This contributs to Learning Disabilities and a range of disorders.

Physically challenged individuals often struggle to navigate
through the various government schemes available to them due to
lack of awareness or accessibility issues.


Physically challenged individuals may face limitations in accessing
traditional skill development opportunities orparticipating
in community activities.

Physically challenged individuals often feel isolated and
struggle to find a supportive community where they can share
their experiences and challenges.

Many individuals with physical challenges struggle to find accessible
fitness and wellness resources that cater to their specific needs and
abilities. They may feel excluded from mainstream fitness programs
and lack guidance on how to maintain their health and well-being.
